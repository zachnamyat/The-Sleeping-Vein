SUBJECT: A complete 16×16 pixel-art tile atlas for "The Emberforge Strata" biome — the sixth stratum, a magma-heated industrial zone of Pyrenkin forge-architecture; basalt floors, forge-iron walls, geothermal vents, ember-glow steel arches, and ribbons of slow-flowing magma.

STYLE: 16-bit pixel art, top-down 2D with a slight 3/4 lean. Hard pixel edges, nearest-neighbor scaling, no anti-aliasing, no gradient ramps, no soft shadows. Inspired by Core Keeper / Stardew Valley / Songs of Conquest.

COMPOSITION: 256×256, 16×16 grid of 16×16-pixel cells with no gaps.

SIZE: Generate 1024×1024 source, downsample 4× nearest-neighbor to 256×256.

ROW LAYOUT (see docs/design/00_tile_atlas_spec.md §2):
- Row 0  : Floor variants — 8 base basalt-tile floors + 8 wear variants (heat-cracks, soot streaks, ember dust).
- Rows 1–2: 47-tile Wang autotile (basalt-floor to forge-iron-wall transitions).
- Row 3  : Wall caps — dark forge-iron silhouettes with hint of inner ember-glow at edge.
- Row 4  : Wall bases — riveted forge-iron plate walls, Pyrenkin industrial seams.
- Row 5  : Ore tiles — Ember-iron (tier-6, lore: "iron annealed in Aphelion-heat") in 8 mining sub-stages, plus 8 variants for raw magma-crystal.
- Row 6  : Small decoration tiles — Pyrenkin forge-bellows, half-buried hammers, glowing coal piles, scattered slag chunks.
- Row 7  : Small decoration tiles — Pyrenkin cultural artifacts (forge-mark brands, broken anvils, ceremonial smith-tongs).
- Row 8  : Liquid surface — slow-flowing magma; viscous, bright orange-red, with crusty cooling edges.
- Row 9  : Liquid edge transitions — magma meets basalt.
- Row 10 : Hazard / trap tiles — geothermal vents (burst-fire), heat-shimmer tiles, cooling-crust thin floors.
- Row 11 : Mural fragments — 8 tiles forming one Pyrenkin forge-mural of Skoldur in his pre-corruption guardianship (lore/05_aeon_sovereigns_bosses.md#boss-7).
- Row 12 : Furniture & door components — heavy iron forge-doors, bellows-tube fittings, anvil surfaces.
- Row 13 : Built-by-player variants — clean ember-iron plate, regular forge-mark inlays.
- Row 14 : VFX overlay tiles — ember sparks, heat-haze shimmer overlays, magma-glow halos.
- Row 15 : Reserved blank — solid #FF00FF.

PALETTE: Restrict to the Emberforge ramp + black + magenta:
  #1a0808  (deepest forge-shadow)
  #3d150b  (dark forge-iron)
  #7a2814  (mid forge-red)
  #c44a1d  (lit ember)
  #f08a2e  (bright fire)
  #f8d167  (brightest molten highlight)
  #000000  (outline only)
  #FF00FF  (Row 15 only)

LORE: The Emberforge Strata is a magma-heated zone of Pyrenkin forge-architecture — the Pyrenkin were industrial smiths who served the Aphelion Wardens and worked the forges that shaped Tideglass and Aphelion-iron. Their forges have not stopped since the Inversion. The Aeon Sovereign Skoldur the Forge-Forsaken rules here; he is the only Sovereign who recognizes returning Echo-Walkers. See lore/04_biomes.md#stratum-6--the-emberforge-strata.

ANTI-PROMPT: photorealism, soft shadows, blur, anti-aliasing, gradient skies, sub-pixel detail, watermark, signature, text labels, frame, border, isometric perspective, gaps between cells.
