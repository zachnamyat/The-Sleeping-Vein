2. **Mira-no-Last-Name** (Storage clerk):
   - Slim, pale, asymmetrical haircut (left side shaved short, right side longer braid). Practical clothes — neutral linen tunic over slim trousers, leather belt with many small pouches. Quiet expressive face. Wears a single small Aphelion-gold pendant.

   - File: assets/sprites/npcs/mira.png

3. **The Cantor of Five Bells** (Musician + Sovereign-Naming sidequest):
   - Tall, slender, ageless-looking. Long flowing robes in five colors corresponding to the Aeon Sovereigns' bells — patches of Root Hollows gold, Glasswright blue, Verdancy green, Drowned cyan, Emberforge red sewn into a single long coat. Carries a small set of five bells on a chest-harness. Hair pure white, tied back with a salt-cord.
   - Palette: AAP-64 + 5 biome accent ramps used as patch-colors.
   - File: assets/sprites/npcs/cantor_of_five_bells.png

4. **Old Hask** (Fishing merchant, Drowned Aphelion-aligned):
   - Weather-beaten old man. Salt-grey beard, heavy fisher's coat in dark Drowned-blue, leather waders, wide-brimmed waxed hat. Carries a small lantern at his belt. One eye milky from a salt-blinding decades ago. Walks slow.
   - Palette: AAP-64 + Drowned Aphelion accents (#15527a, #2c8fb8, #6cd3e1) on coat.
   - File: assets/sprites/npcs/old_hask.png

5. **The Veiled Buyer** (Random-spawn cloaked merchant):
   - Tall, fully cloaked figure. Black hood pulled forward; no face visible — only a faint Aphelion-gold gleam where the eyes would be. Robe layered with small Vesari ship-tokens (he buys curios). Voice/identity is canonically ambiguous.
   - Palette: AAP-64 deep blacks/dark greys + Final Spiral gold gleam. Vesari salt-pale accents on tokens.
