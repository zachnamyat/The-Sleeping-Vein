# Hero asset — 2 polish passes recommended.

SUBJECT: Echo-Walker attack-swing sprite sheets across 4 weapon classes — melee (one-handed sword), ranged (bow draw + release), magic (staff casting), summoning (calling-gesture). The Walker is the same baseline figure as walker_default.txt (bald, plain attire, hand-glow).

STYLE: (see prompts/_STYLE.txt)

COMPOSITION: Generate FOUR separate sprite sheets, one per weapon class. Each sheet is a 4-direction × 4-frame attack-swing animation laid horizontally:
- Frames 0–3   : Attack, facing DOWN — 4-frame swing
- Frames 4–7   : Attack, facing UP
- Frames 8–11  : Attack, facing LEFT
- Frames 12–15 : Attack, facing RIGHT
Each frame is 16×24 pixels (feet at row 23). Final sheet per weapon class: 256×24. No gaps.

For melee: the Walker's right hand holds the weapon (no glow); for ranged: bow drawn and released; for magic: staff held forward, glyph appears at staff tip on frame 3; for summoning: open empty hand with growing gold-light ball.

SIZE: Per weapon class, generate at 4096×384 source. Downsample 16× nearest-neighbor to 256×24 final.

PALETTE: Same as walker_default.txt — AAP-64 character tones + Final Spiral gold for any magic VFX. Weapons get tier-1 base materials (wood haft + grey iron blade for sword, oiled wood for bow, plain wood + crystal-tip for staff). Background #FF00FF (keyed out).

LORE: Walkers attack by anchoring physical motion to their resonance; the gold hand-glow brightens slightly on impact frame (frame 2 of 4). See lore/07_echo_walkers_player.md and docs/reference/core-keeper-mechanics.md combat section.

ANIMATION: 4 frames at 16 fps:
- F0: wind-up pose
- F1: mid-swing
- F2: impact pose (peak motion, brightest hand-glow)
- F3: follow-through / recovery

POST-GENERATION (per weapon class):

- Key out magenta.
