SUBJECT: Echo-Walker character sprite sheet — the player character. A bald, humanoid figure of indeterminate ancestry (the Aphelion's eroded human template), wearing plain unmarked attire in soft natural fibers (Aphelion-template default — no faction markings, no armor). Front, back, and side views. Their right hand emits a faint golden Aphelion-light glow when not holding a tool.

STYLE: (see prompts/_STYLE.txt)

COMPOSITION: A horizontal sprite sheet covering idle and walk animations across 4 facing directions. Layout (left-to-right):
- Frames 0–3   : Idle, facing DOWN (toward camera) — 4-frame breathing loop
- Frames 4–7   : Idle, facing UP
- Frames 8–11  : Idle, facing LEFT
- Frames 12–15 : Idle, facing RIGHT
- Frames 16–21 : Walk, facing DOWN — 6-frame cycle
- Frames 22–27 : Walk, facing UP
- Frames 28–33 : Walk, facing LEFT
- Frames 34–39 : Walk, facing RIGHT
Each frame is exactly 16×24 pixels (feet aligned to row 23, head at row 0). Final sheet: 640×24. No gaps between frames.

SIZE: Generate at 10240×384 source (256× per-frame width × 64× height; effectively 1024×384 per 16-frame strip stacked OR one mega-strip — pick whichever Gemini handles best). Downsample 16× nearest-neighbor for a final 640×24 sheet. If single-shot 640×24 is impossible at scale, generate 4 strips (one per direction × idle/walk pair) at 256×24 each and concatenate horizontally.

PALETTE: AAP-64 skin and natural-fiber tones for body + clothing. Hand-of-light glow uses Final Spiral gold ramp (#a87f24, #dab14a, #f8e088). Outline #000000 sparingly. Background #FF00FF (keyed out).

LORE: The Walker is bald, humanoid, plain in attire. The Aphelion's template has drifted across uncounted Beats — different Walkers look different, none match any surviving culture. The right hand glows golden when empty — the visible signature of the Aphelion-sliver they were spun from. See lore/07_echo_walkers_player.md.

ANIMATION:
- Idle (4 frames, 8 fps per direction): subtle breathing — chest rise/fall + hand-glow pulse sync to a slow rhythm.
- Walk (6 frames, 12 fps per direction): 2-step cycle (left foot forward, mid, right foot forward, mid). Feet stay on row 23. Arms swing slightly counter to legs.

- Verify each 16-pixel column slice is a clean frame; feet at row 23 in every frame.
- Key out magenta.
