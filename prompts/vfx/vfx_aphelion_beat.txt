# Ticket 1.7 + 1.10 — Aphelion Beat pulse VFX (global ambient effect)

SUBJECT: A pixel-art VFX overlay — a soft golden ring-pulse that emanates from screen-edges and the Walker's hand, synced to the 23-second Aphelion Beat cycle. Used as a subtle ambient overlay layer over gameplay.

STYLE: (see prompts/_STYLE.txt)

COMPOSITION: A horizontal sprite strip of 6 frames at 32×32 each. Final: 192×32. The pulse builds and decays over 23 seconds — frame 0 is the start (faint), frame 2 is the peak, frame 5 is the dim recovery before the next Beat begins.

Each frame shows a single soft golden ring expanding outward, with subtle gold-mote particles drifting along the ring. The ring is intentionally low-opacity so it doesn't dominate gameplay.

SIZE: Generate at 3072×512 source. Downsample 16× nearest-neighbor to 192×32 final.

PALETTE: Final Spiral gold ramp (#a87f24 → #dab14a → #f8e088) for the ring + AAP-64 gentle yellows for motes. Background #FF00FF.

LORE: The Aphelion Beat is the 23-second pulse of the captive sun. Audio cue lives in 1.10. The visual Beat is felt: hand-of-light VFX intensifies on Beat, Aphelion-sliver readout shimmers, certain mob types (Hollowling-infected) flicker their eye-glow on Beat. See lore/01_cosmology_and_universe.md and lore/07_echo_walkers_player.md.

ANIMATION: 6 frames at sync to 23-second Beat (effective FPS varies; Antigravity should drive frame index from a Beat-cycle timer in code, not a fixed FPS).
