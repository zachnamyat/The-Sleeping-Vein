SUBJECT: A pixel-art sheet of 9 status-effect icons for the HUD BuffStrip in The Sleeping Vein. Output as a 3×3 grid of 16×16 cells (final 48×48), one icon per cell, magenta background.

STYLE: (see prompts/_STYLE.txt) 16-bit pixel art, hard pixel edges, no anti-aliasing. Each icon is symbolic, readable at 14×14 px on a HUD strip — bold silhouette over restricted palette.

COMPOSITION: A 3-column × 3-row grid of 16×16 cells = total source canvas 48×48 (we will downsample 32× from 1024×1024). Each cell holds a SINGLE symbolic icon centered with 1-2px of magenta padding. Background of every cell is pure magenta (#FF00FF). Each cell has a thin 1-px dark outline on its symbol so it reads against any HUD colour.

  (1,0) FREEZE: a full ice-cube silhouette, blocky pale-blue with a single highlight pixel
  (1,1) STUN: a gold star cluster (3-4 small overlapping stars), yellow-gold with darker outline
  (1,2) SLOW: a downward grey arrow with two short slow-mo lines after it (motion-blur dashes)
  (2,0) BLEED: a red blood-drop with a thin dark outline, sharply tapered top
  (2,1) CONFUSION: a violet question-mark "?" glyph, slightly tilted
  (2,2) SHOCK: a bright cyan-white lightning bolt zig-zag

SIZE: Output 1536×1536 source (so each 16×16 cell starts at 512×512). Downsample 32× nearest-neighbor to 48×48 final sheet.
