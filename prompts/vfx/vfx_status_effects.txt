# Tickets 2.11 + 6.6 + 6.7 + 6.8 + 6.9 + 6.13 — Combat VFX (damage flash + DoT overlays + status effect overlays)

SUBJECT: A pixel-art sheet of small VFX overlay sprites for combat status feedback: damage flash, burn, poison, cold, freeze, stun.

STYLE: (see prompts/_STYLE.txt)

COMPOSITION: A 6×4 grid of 16×16 cells (each VFX is a 4-frame strip). Final sheet: 96×64. Layout (one row per VFX, 4 frames per row):

Row 0 — Damage Flash: 4-frame white-pulse overlay (frame 0: full white, frame 1: 80% white, frame 2: 40% white, frame 3: fade-out).
Row 1 — Burn: 4-frame ember-flame mote that lifts upward off the affected entity.
Row 2 — Poison: 4-frame green spore puffs (alternating dense/sparse).
Row 3 — Cold: 4-frame frost-shimmer overlay with snowflake-mote drift.
Row 4 — Freeze: 4-frame ice-block encasement (frame 0: thin ice forming, frame 3: fully encased translucent block).
Row 5 — Stun: 4-frame yellow lightning-bolt circle rotating around the target's head.

Each frame fills its 16×16 cell with the VFX content (the target it overlays is not drawn — these are pure overlays).

SIZE: Generate at 1536×1024 source. Downsample 16× nearest-neighbor to 96×64 final.

PALETTE: VFX-specific:
- Damage flash: pure white #FFFFFF + slight #FFE0E0 tint on edges.
- Burn: Emberforge ramp (#c44a1d, #f08a2e, #f8d167).
- Poison: Verdancy ramp (#3e7a3b, #7bbf64, #c0e08a) for the spore puffs.
- Cold: Drowned Aphelion ramp light end + AAP-64 whites (#6cd3e1, #cdf7f3, #FFFFFF).
- Freeze: Drowned Aphelion ramp full + slight white core.
- Stun: Final Spiral gold ramp + AAP-64 yellow.
Background #FF00FF (keyed out).

LORE: Status effects map 1:1 to Core Keeper mechanics with lore-flavor names per docs/design/02_lore_to_mechanics_mapping.md.

POST-GENERATION:
- Downsample 96×64 nearest-neighbor.
- Slice into 6 horizontal 64×16 sub-strips (one per VFX), saved separately:
  - assets/sprites/vfx/damage_flash.png (64×16, 4 frames)
  - assets/sprites/vfx/status_burn.png (64×16, 4 frames)
  - assets/sprites/vfx/status_poison.png (64×16, 4 frames)
  - assets/sprites/vfx/status_cold.png (64×16, 4 frames)
  - assets/sprites/vfx/status_freeze.png (64×16, 4 frames)
  - assets/sprites/vfx/status_stun.png (64×16, 4 frames)
- Wrap each as SpriteFrames at resources/sprites/vfx_<name>.tres with one looping animation.
- Manifest entries: vfx_damage_flash, vfx_status_burn, vfx_status_poison, vfx_status_cold, vfx_status_freeze, vfx_status_stun. Status "final".
